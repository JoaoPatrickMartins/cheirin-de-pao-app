| AdminLayout guard | A primeira linha de defesa do painel -- redirect para '/' se nao ADMIN |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-07-12-01 | Tampering | Estorno sem confirmacao visual | mitigate | Dialog de confirmacao com role=dialog aria-modal=true obrigatorio antes de chamar POST /admin/payments/:id/refund |
